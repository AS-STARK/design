PRESENT SITUATION

  • ~1 Billion of us living in extreme poverty.
  • No truly global platform to address the problem at global scale using technology.
  • Resources available but lack of human consciousness to help our fellow friends left behind.
  • NGOs and corporate dedicated to solve the issue but operating in silos without lack of operating system.
  • Many of us involved to help but one off support not effective to take families out of poverty trap.
